Quick Answers

Everything New Players Usually Ask Before Entering

We kept the most common questions here so visitors can understand the experience, group size, atmosphere, and timing without guessing.

What is an escape room?
An escape room is a live puzzle adventure where players solve clues, unlock secrets, and work together to complete the mission before time runs out.
How many players can join?
Most rooms work best with 2 to 6 players, depending on the theme and difficulty level.
How long does the game last?
Most escape experiences last around 60 minutes, with a little extra time for briefing and wrap-up.
Is it scary?
Some rooms are intense and atmospheric, but not every experience is horror-based. You can choose the mood that fits your group.
