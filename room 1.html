Pre-Game Briefing

Briefing File 01

You have been wrongly imprisoned inside a high-security facility. The guards will return in 60 minutes. Your only chance is to escape before lockdown.

Your team starts with limited information, blocked pathways, and only the clues hidden inside the wing. Every object, pattern, and strange detail could be part of the breakout route.

Pressure Notes

Expect dim lighting, echoing footsteps, and distant alarm sounds. Every second increases tension as the countdown gets closer.

If You Don't Escape...

Lockdown Outcome

The alarm will trigger, guards will return, and your escape attempt will be locked down. But don't worry, you can always try again and beat the challenge.

Even if the countdown wins this round, your team still learns the room flow, clue rhythm, and where the real pressure points begin. The next attempt always feels sharper.
